## Research Pillars

### 1. Clinical AI Diagnostics & Psychometrics
Developing the **"Therapeutic Interpretability"** framework to treat model behavior as clinical pathology. Core concepts include SCAI Risk (Seemingly Conscious AI), Epistemic Coercion, and Dissociative Reasoning in LLMs. Applications include the **American Emotional Infrastructure (AEI)** project ‚Äî socio-technical resilience models and anti-addiction design.

### 2. Agentic Systems & Reliability
Defining governance and safety standards for multi-agent swarms and autonomous systems. Focus areas: agentic maturity models, cascade failure analysis, statefulness evaluation, and multi-agent penetration testing.

### 3. Adversarial ML & Mechanistic Interpretability
Using circuit-level insights to constrain high-entropy model behaviors. Research includes transformer exploit taxonomies (attention hijacking, representation poisoning) and safety through interpretability with hardware-rooted security for quantum-adjacent systems.
